Hierarchical Energy-Aware Job Dispatch Using Reinforcement Learning Framework for Airport Electric Ground Support Equipment

Taeho Jeong, Jaemin Park, Jaehyun Park, Donghyun Kim, Vishvesh Khanzode, Seockcheon Lee · Working Paper · Nov 2025

"How can we optimize the scheduling of electric ground support equipment at airports while balancing energy efficiency and operational constraints?"

This work proposes a hierarchical reinforcement learning framework for dispatching electric ground support equipment (eGSE) at airports. The framework addresses the complex scheduling problem while considering energy consumption patterns, charging infrastructure constraints, and real-time operational demands.
